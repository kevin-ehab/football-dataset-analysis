Dataset Description¶

This is an analysis for a European soccer dataset which contains columns for:

  • Country Id
  • League Id
  • Season
  • Stage
  • Date
  • Match Api Id
  • Home Team Api Id
  • Away Team Api Id
  • Home Team Goal
  • Away Team Goal
  • Player ids

Question(s) for Analysis¶

  • What is the league with the least and most total goals per match?
  • What is the league with the lowest and highest percentage of draws per game?
  • How does scoring home goals correlate to the scoring of the away goals?
  • Do home teams win more games than away teams?
  • Do later season matches have more or less goals?

Conclusions of the research questions:¶

  • Research Question 1 (What is the league with the least and most total goals per match?)

    The league with the least goals indicates either the weakest attackers or strongest defenders and vice versa. So the league with the strogest defenders or weakest attackers is Poland Ekstraklasa while the team with the strongest attackers or weakest defenders is Netherlands Eredivisie.

  • Research Question 2 (What is the league with the lowest and highest percentage of draws per game?)

    The league with the most draws indicates the league with the least excitement for fans and vice versa. So the Most exciting league for fans is Spain liga while the least is France league 1.

  • Research Question 3 (How does scoring home goals correlate to the scoring of the away goals ?)

    The resulted correlation is negative. So as The home goals increase, the away goals decrease. This indicates that as the score gets higher, the away teams tend to give up.

  • Research Question 4 (Do home teams win more games than away teams?)

    The result indicates that teams playing at their stadiums tend to have an advantage over the teams playing away. This result can be due to the presence of the home team supporters in larger quantities

  • Research Question 5 (Do later season matches have more or less goals?)

    The result indicates that at the end of the season, the teams' managers take a more defensive approach as they want to not lose their current position and win without too much risk.
